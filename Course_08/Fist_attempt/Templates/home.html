Twinning research

Discover groundbreaking scientific papers for twins research.

You can find articles not only about twins but also about the fertility cycle and the relationship between them.

Motivation

The study of the difference between fraternal twins in humans and sheep is an interesting and relevant topic. It can lead to insights into the genetic and environmental factors that play a role in the development of individual differences, which in turn can contribute to the understanding of hereditary disorders and the development of treatments.
